## Getting Started

Welcome to Melodic Drum Trainer, an interactive drum practice application designed to help you build rhythm stability, limb independence, and master common grooves and rudiments.

### First Launch
1. Launch the app on your iPad
2. Grant microphone and audio permissions when prompted
3. Connect your MIDI drum device (optional but recommended)
4. Complete the initial setup wizard

### System Requirements
- iOS 16.0 or later
- iPadOS 16.0 or later
- Compatible MIDI drum device (recommended)
- Wired headphones or speakers for best audio experience

## MIDI Device Setup

### Automatic Connection
1. Connect your MIDI drum device via USB or Bluetooth
2. The app will automatically detect most standard drum devices
3. Connection status appears in the top-right corner

### Manual MIDI Mapping
If your device isn't automatically recognized:
1. Go to Settings → MIDI Configuration
2. Tap "Manual Mapping"
3. Hit each drum pad and assign it to the corresponding drum sound
4. Test the mapping with the verification tool

### Supported Devices
- Roland TD series
- Alesis electronic drums
- Yamaha DTX series
- Most USB/MIDI compatible drum devices

### Connection Troubleshooting
- **USB**: Use a USB-C to USB adapter for iPad connection
- **Bluetooth**: Ensure device is in pairing mode
- **Latency**: Use wired connections for best timing accuracy

## Practice Modes

### Performance Mode
Complete songs at full tempo to test your skills.
- Play at original BPM
- Real-time scoring (0-100%)
- Star ratings: 50%=1★, 75%=2★, 90%=3★, 100%=Platinum★
- Unlock Memory Mode with 100% score

### Practice Mode
Slow down and focus on difficult sections.
- **BPM Control**: Adjust tempo from 50-200 BPM
- **Auto Speed-Up**: Automatically increases by 10 BPM when playing well
- **Loop Regions**: Set start/end points to repeat difficult sections
- **Wait Mode**: Pauses at target notes until you play correctly

### Memory Mode
Advanced practice without visual guides.
- Unlocked after achieving 100% in Performance Mode
- Visual cues gradually fade away
- Audio backing track and metronome remain active
- Earn Black Star★ for 100% completion

## Content Browser

### Finding Lessons
- **Categories**: Browse by skill level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
- **Tags**: Filter by style (Rock, Jazz, Latin, etc.)
- **Search**: Find specific songs or techniques
- **Difficulty**: Color-coded difficulty indicators

### Lesson Structure
- **Courses**: Multi-lesson progressions around specific concepts
- **Individual Lessons**: Single practice sessions
- **Steps**: Progressive difficulty within each lesson

### Content Preview
- Tap any lesson to hear an audio preview
- View lesson details, BPM, and time signature
- Check your previous scores and progress

## Progress Tracking

### Daily Goals
- Default goal: 5 minutes of practice per day
- Customize in Settings → Goals
- Track streaks and maintain consistency

### Achievements
- **Stars**: Earned for lesson completion scores
- **Trophies**: Milestone achievements for total practice time
- **Streaks**: Consecutive days meeting daily goals
- **Black Stars**: Perfect scores in Memory Mode

### User Levels
- Gain XP for completing lessons and achieving high scores
- Level progression unlocks new content and features
- View detailed statistics in Progress tab

### Progress Sync
- Automatic CloudKit synchronization across devices
- Backup and restore progress data
- Offline practice with sync when connected

## Settings & Customization

### Audio Settings
- **Output Device**: Choose speakers or headphones
- **Latency Compensation**: Adjust for audio delay (0-100ms)
- **Metronome Volume**: Independent volume control
- **Metronome Sounds**: 6 different click sounds available

### Visual Settings
- **High Contrast Mode**: White feedback with icons for accessibility
- **Combo Flash**: Enable/disable perfect hit visual effects
- **Lane Colors**: Customize drum lane appearance
- **Note Preview**: Adjust upcoming note visibility

### Practice Settings
- **Timing Windows**: Adjust Perfect/Early/Late thresholds
- **Scoring**: Customize penalty weights for different errors
- **Auto-Pause**: Pause on mistakes in Practice Mode
- **Count-In**: Set measure count before lesson starts

### Accessibility
- **VoiceOver**: Full screen reader support
- **Large Text**: Dynamic type support
- **Reduced Motion**: Minimize animations
- **Color Blind Support**: High contrast and icon modes

## Troubleshooting

### Audio Issues
**No Sound**
- Check device volume and mute switch
- Verify audio output device in Settings
- Restart the app if audio engine fails

**Audio Delay/Latency**
- Use wired headphones instead of Bluetooth
- Adjust latency compensation in Settings
- Close other audio apps running in background

### MIDI Issues
**Device Not Detected**
- Check USB/Bluetooth connection
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting
- Use manual MIDI mapping if auto-detection fails

**Incorrect Mapping**
- Go to Settings → MIDI Configuration
- Reset and reconfigure device mapping
- Test each pad individually

### Performance Issues
**App Crashes**
- Force close and restart the app
- Restart your iPad
- Check available storage space (need 1GB+ free)

**Slow Performance**
- Close other apps running in background
- Reduce visual effects in Settings
- Lower audio quality if needed

### Sync Issues
**Progress Not Syncing**
- Check internet connection
- Sign in to iCloud in iPad Settings
- Force sync in Settings → Cloud Sync

**Missing Lessons**
- Check internet connection for content download
- Verify sufficient storage space
- Try refreshing content in Settings

- In-app help: Settings → Help & Support
- Video tutorials: Available in the Learn tab
- Contact support: settings → Contact Support
