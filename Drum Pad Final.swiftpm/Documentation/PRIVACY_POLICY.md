# Privacy Policy - Melodic Drum Trainer

**Effective Date**: December 16, 2024  
**Last Updated**: December 16, 2024

## Introduction

AudioKit Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Melodic Drum Trainer application ("the App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Practice Data**: Lesson progress, scores, and practice session statistics
- **Settings and Preferences**: Audio settings, visual preferences, and practice configurations
- **Custom Content**: MIDI files and lessons you import or create

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: iPad model, iOS version, and app version for compatibility
- **Usage Data**: App performance metrics and crash reports (anonymized)
- **Audio Calibration**: Microphone input for latency calibration (processed locally, not stored)

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Contacts or photos
- Browsing history or activity outside the app
- Audio recordings of your practice sessions

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Practice Progress**: Track your learning progress and achievements
- **Personalization**: Customize the app experience based on your preferences
- **Performance**: Optimize app performance and fix technical issues
- **Synchronization**: Sync your data across your devices (with your consent)

### We Do NOT Use Your Information For
- Advertising or marketing purposes
- Selling to third parties
- Creating user profiles for commercial purposes
- Tracking your behavior outside the app

## Data Storage and Synchronization

### Local Storage
- All practice data is stored locally on your device by default
- Data is encrypted using iOS standard encryption methods
- You maintain full control over your local data

### CloudKit Synchronization (Optional)
- **Opt-in Only**: CloudKit sync requires your explicit consent
- **Apple's Infrastructure**: Data is synced through Apple's CloudKit service
- **End-to-End Encryption**: Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Your Apple ID**: Sync is tied to your Apple ID, not our servers

### What Gets Synced
When you enable CloudKit sync:
- Practice progress and achievements
- Custom lesson content you create
- App settings and preferences
- Practice session history

### What Doesn't Get Synced
- Audio calibration data
- Temporary files and caches
- Debug information
- Device-specific settings

## Third-Party Services

### AudioKit Framework
- **Purpose**: Audio processing and MIDI handling
- **Data**: No personal data is shared with AudioKit
- **Local Processing**: All audio processing happens on your device

### Apple Services
- **CloudKit**: Optional data synchronization (with your consent)
- **App Store**: Standard app distribution and updates
- **TestFlight**: Beta testing (if you participate)

### No Other Third Parties
- We do not integrate with social media platforms
- We do not use analytics services that track users
- We do not share data with advertising networks

## Your Rights and Controls

### Data Access
- View all your practice data within the app
- Export your progress data through Settings
- Access your CloudKit data through Apple's privacy tools

### Data Control
- **Delete Local Data**: Clear all app data through iOS Settings
- **Disable Sync**: Turn off CloudKit sync at any time
- **Export Data**: Export your practice data for backup or transfer

### Account Deletion
- **No Account Required**: The app doesn't require account creation
- **CloudKit Data**: Disable sync to stop storing data in CloudKit
- **Complete Removal**: Delete the app to remove all local data

## Data Security

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted using iOS standard methods
- **Local Processing**: Audio and MIDI processing happens on-device
- **Secure Transmission**: CloudKit uses Apple's secure infrastructure
- **No Remote Storage**: We don't operate our own servers storing user data

### Access Controls
- **Device Security**: Protected by your device's passcode/biometrics
- **App Permissions**: Minimal permissions requested (microphone for calibration only)
- **Keychain Storage**: Sensitive settings stored in iOS keychain

## Children's Privacy

### Age-Appropriate Design
- The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages
- No personal information collection from users of any age
- No social features or communication capabilities
- Educational focus with no inappropriate content

### COPPA Compliance
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- No account creation or personal data entry required
- Parents can review and delete any data through device settings

## International Users

### Data Processing
- Data processing occurs on your device (local-first design)
- CloudKit data is processed by Apple according to their privacy policy
- No cross-border data transfers by our company

### Regional Compliance
- **GDPR (EU)**: Right to access, rectify, and delete data
- **CCPA (California)**: Right to know, delete, and opt-out
- **Other Jurisdictions**: Committed to respecting local privacy laws
